Arthur Morgan:

Background/Early Life

Arthur Morgan was born in 1863 in the Nothern US to Beatrice and Lyle Morgan. His mother died when Arthur was young and his father was a petty criminal. When Arthur was 11 he wathced his father get taken into custody and eventually killed.

Time with Dutch

At the age of 14, Arthur was found by a man named Dutch Vander Linde. Dutch along with his buisness partner Hosea Mathhews, raised Arthur and integrated him into the gang. Arthur would serve Dutch and aid in multiple crimes including bank roberries and train heists.

Relationships

Arthur Morgan fell in love with a woman named Mary Gellis. The two would end their relationship due to Mary's father's disdain for Arthur. Later, Arthur would meet a woman named Eliza who he would impregnate. Eliza was aware of Arthur's life of crime and Arthur agreed to due all he could to support Eliza and their son Isaac. One day, Athur went to visit Eliza and Isaac and saw two graves. Eliza and Isaac were killed over $10 by Raiders.
